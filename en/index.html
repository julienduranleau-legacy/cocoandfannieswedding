You are cordially invited to
Our Wedding

Come celebrate with us this August in the land of mooses and maple leaves. We promise good fun, dancing, amazing food and glorious deserts. Bring your smiles and your lovely presence and let’s have a party worth having!

When

The Wedding will take place on Sunday the 27th of August 2017. Guests can arrive starting at 2 PM and the ceremony will start at 3 PM.

Where

The ceremony and reception will both take place at the “Pavillon de la Jamaïque” on Notre Dame Island, in Montréal Canada. 1 Circuit Gilles Vileneuve, Montréal, Canada

Dinner

Drinks, hors d’oeuvres and bubbly will be served after the ceremony. The meal itself will be a buffet of a wide variety of dishes from a local restaurant. Sangria, wine and beer will be available.

STYLE SCOOP

Here is a sample of the wedding colors. Guests are free to decide to try and match it or not, all that we want is for people to be comfy and happy. Dress code is whatever you want it to be!

1000 LUCKY BIRDS

It is a Japanese tradition to fold 1000 origami cranes to bring luck to a loved one. Following this tradition, the Nakase clan has painstakingly folded 1000 little paper birds for each of their weddings and it was important for us to continue with the tradition. Our paper cranes will be displayed at the reception and surrounded by more paper for guests to try the art of folding themselves!
